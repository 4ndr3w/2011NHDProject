John Robert Lewis

John Robert Lewis was born in Pike County, Al. in 1940. He was the third of ten children to be born to his parents, who were sharecroppers. Lewis led a hard life on the plantation, picking over 200 Lbs. of cotton a day. In 1960, he helped to form the Student Nonviolent Coordinating Committee (SNCC) and then went on to become chairman of the SNCC in 1963.
